Micaela Linares

is a professional makeup artist with more than 6 years of experience. She trained at the Kryolan school, Peru and then specialized in Argentina and Colombia.

Her career has been developed in various scenarios, such as: social, weddings, editorials, retail and advertising. She has collaborated with international brands such as Nike, Adidas, Caterpillar, H&M, Claro. Likewise, with Koketa, Exit, Fina, Mentha and Chocolate, peruvian brands.

Micaela has worked with leading photographers Yukimi Moromisato, Alessandro Gogny, Armando Rodríguez, Lalo Rondón. For her, it is extremely important to convey the essence of the brand or model in each session, trying to turn makeup into its own expressive and diverse form.
